FAQ

Answers to common questions about our fun travel guides.

Are these guides free?

Yes, all guides on USA Travel & Food Guide are completely free to read! They are made by fans of travel for everyone to enjoy.

How do I save my plans?

Your plans, like your visited places and food lists, are automatically saved in your browser. If you clear your browser's data, your plans will be reset, so be careful!

Why isn't the guide loading?

Please make sure you have a good internet connection and are using a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Try refreshing the page!

How often are new guides added?

A new City and a new Food guide are available every day! Check back daily for a new challenge.

Is an account required?

No account is needed to read our guides. Just visit the site and start reading instantly!

How can I give you an idea for a guide?

We'd love to hear from you! Please use the "Connect With Us" contact form on our home page to send us your awesome ideas.

Are you part of a big travel company?

No, USA Travel & Food Guide is an independent, fan-made website and is not affiliated with any major travel agencies.

What can I read these guides on?

Our guides are built to work on most modern computers, tablets, and mobile phones. Just open your browser and you're ready to go!
