Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

USA Travel & Food Guide ("we", "us", "our") is a website with fun travel guides. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and guides (the "Service"). We do not require you to sign up for an account and we collect as little data as possible.

Information We Handle

  • Travel Data: Your travel statistics (like visited places and food counts) are stored in your browser’s local storage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Contact Information: If you choose to contact us via our contact form, we receive the information you send, such as your name and email address, so we can write back to you.

Data Security

We are committed to keeping our travelers' data safe. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, your travel data is as secure as your own device.

Children's Privacy

Our guides are made for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our website uses your browser's `localStorage` to save your travel progress and stats. This is for the guide to work and is cleared if you clear your browser's data.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data at any time to remove all locally stored travel statistics.
  • You can use your browser's private or incognito mode to browse without saving any data on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will let you know of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend checking this page every once in a while.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact form on our Home page.
